I found this genuine 60's embroidery kit in my grandmothers basement after she passed away. I was in awe by the bright colors and asked my mom if I could have it. The 1960's patterns are perfect if you enjoy a retro touch in your home and some fun color combinations. You can try visiting flea markets or search the second-hand webshops for original embroidery kits. Another – perhaps easier – way is to look for 1960's needlework or embroidery books and then collect the required material on your own.
